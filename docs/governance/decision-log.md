## 2025

### December

**License Transition to Apache 2.0 / CERN-OHL-P**
- **Date:** December 2025
- **Decision:** Transition from AGPL-3.0 to Apache 2.0 (software) and CERN-OHL-P (hardware)
- **Rationale:** Enable clinical translation, academic partnerships, and 3D Slicer integration
- **Status:** Planning phase
- **RFC:** [RFC-001](../../rfcs/RFC-001-license-transition.md)

**GitHub Organization Restructuring**
- **Date:** December 2025
- **Decision:** Separate hardware/software repos, create community infrastructure
- **Rationale:** Clear contribution pathways, professional organization
- **Status:** In progress (Week 1 of 8)
- **RFC:** Pending

### November

**Community Governance Model**
- **Date:** November 2025
- **Decision:** Adopt TSC governance inspired by CNCF/Linux Foundation
- **Rationale:** Scalable, proven model for open-source projects
- **Status:** Established
- **Document:** [TSC Charter](tsc-charter.md)
