## üéØ Contribution Levels

We recognize different levels of contribution:

### ü•â Contributor
- 1+ merged pull request
- OR 1+ helpful issue report
- OR participation in community discussions

### ü•à Regular Contributor
- 5+ merged pull requests
- OR significant documentation contributions
- OR active community participation over 3+ months

### ü•á Core Contributor
- 25+ merged pull requests
- AND sustained participation over 6+ months
- AND mentoring of new contributors

### üíé Committer
- Core contributor status
- AND TSC approval for commit access
- AND demonstrated technical excellence
- AND alignment with community values

## üìù Contributor Agreement

All contributors must agree to our [Contributor License Agreement (CLA)](https://github.com/OpenwaterHealth/openwater-commons/blob/main/docs/governance/CLA.md).

**What does the CLA do?**
- Protects contributors with patent grants
- Enables project licensing decisions
- Ensures we can distribute your work
- Standard practice in open source

**What doesn't it do?**
- Transfer copyright (you keep it!)
- Limit your use of your contributions
- Give exclusive rights to Openwater
- Force future contributions
