## Discussions

### 1. GitHub Organization Restructuring

**Background:**
- Current 48 repos in flat structure
- Hardware/software mixed in repos
- No community infrastructure

**Decision:**
- Proceed with comprehensive restructuring
- Create 4 infrastructure repos (openwater-commons, .github, awesome-openwater, openwater-docs)
- Separate hardware into dedicated repos
- Target completion: 8 weeks

**Action Items:**

### 2. License Transition

**Background:**
- SlicerOpenLIFU rejected by 3D Slicer due to AGPL
- Medical imaging ecosystem uses permissive licenses
- Blocks academic partnerships

**Decision:**
- Move forward with Apache 2.0 (software) + CERN-OHL-P (hardware)
- Present evidence to board (3D Slicer rejection)
- Target completion: Q1 2026

**Action Items:**
- [ ] Legal: Finalize CLA

- [ ] Aaron: Schedule board presentation

### 3. Community Infrastructure

**Requirements Identified:**
- TSC governance structure
- RFC process for major decisions
- Contribution guidelines
- Community recognition

**Decision:**
- Establish TSC with 9 members (3 Openwater, 3 academic, 3 community)
- Adopt RFC process for major changes
- Create comprehensive contributor recognition

**Action Items:**
