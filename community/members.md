### Contributor
- 1+ merged PR or significant contribution
- Listed in [CONTRIBUTORS.md](../CONTRIBUTORS.md)

### Community Member
- 5+ merged PRs or 3+ months participation
- Listed here
- Access to members-only channels (future)

### Core Contributor
- 25+ merged PRs or exceptional contribution
- Eligible for committer status
- Voting rights in some decisions

### Committer
- Core contributor + TSC approval
- Write access to repositories
- Can merge PRs
